# SEO Copy Changes — For Review

These are proposed meta title/description changes for better Google ranking.
Review each one before applying. The goal is to naturally include target keywords
(knitwear manufacturer, Ludhiana, Punjab, flat-knit) without sounding AI-generated.

---

## Homepage (layout.tsx)

**Current title:**
`GOLFLINE | Premium Flat-Knit Knitwear Manufacturing`

**Proposed title:**
`GOLFLINE — Premium Flat-Knit Knitwear Manufacturer in Ludhiana, Punjab | 35+ Years`

**Why:** Adds geo-targeting (Ludhiana, Punjab) which is critical for "near me" and local searches. Also adds "35+ Years" as a trust signal.

**Current description:**
`With 35+ years of expertise, Golfline is your full-cycle partner for premium flat-knit manufacturing. We transform your design vision into market-ready reality with precision, consistency, and speed.`

**Proposed description:**
`Golfline is a leading flat-knit knitwear manufacturer in Ludhiana, Punjab with 35+ years of expertise. Full-cycle sweater & pullover manufacturing — sampling to dispatch. Stoll & Shima Seiki machines, zero subcontracting, 10-day sample development.`

**Why:** Front-loads "knitwear manufacturer in Ludhiana, Punjab" — Google weighs the first 60 chars heavily. Adds specific differentiators (Stoll/Shima Seiki, 10-day sampling) that competitors won't have.
